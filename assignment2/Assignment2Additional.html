Pasta

  1. Step 1:Boil the Pasta

    1.Take a large pot and fill it with water (around 4-5 cups).
    2️.Add 1 teaspoon salt to the water.
    3️.Bring the water to a boil.
    4️.Add 1 cup of pasta (penne, spaghetti, or any type you like).
    5️.Cook for 8-10 minutes (or follow the package instructions).
    6️.Once done, drain the water using a colander and set the pasta aside.

  2. Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

    1️.Heat 2 tablespoons of oil or butter in a pan.
    2️.Add 3 cloves of chopped garlic and sauté for a few seconds.
    3️.Add ½ cup of chopped onions and cook until soft.
    4️.Add ½ cup of chopped tomatoes (or tomato puree) and stir well.
    5️.Season with ½ teaspoon salt, ½ teaspoon pepper, ½ teaspoon chili flakes, and ½ teaspoon oregano.
    6. Let it cook for 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.

  3. Step 3: Mix Pasta with Sauce

    1.Add the boiled pasta to the pan with the sauce.
    2️.Mix well so the pasta gets coated evenly.
    3️.Add 2 tablespoons of grated cheese (optional).
    4️. Stir everything for 2 minutes on low heat.

  4. Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

    1️. Turn off the heat and transfer the pasta to a plate.
    2️. Garnish with fresh basil or coriander leaves.
    3️. Sprinkle grated cheese on top for extra flavor.
    4️. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pasta!

    Ingrediant List

  • Pasta - 1 cup (penne/spaghetti/maccheroni)

  • Water -4-5 cups

  • Salt - 1 teaspoon (for boiling pasta)

  • Oil / Butter -2 tablespoons

  • Garlic -3 cloves (chopped)

  • Onion - ½ cup (chopped)

  • Tomato / Tomato Puree - ½ cup

  • Black Pepper - ½ teaspoon

  • Chili Flakes - ½ teaspoon

  • Oregano - ½ teaspoon

  • Cheese (Optional) - 2 tablespoons (grated)

  • Fresh Basil / Coriander (for garnish)
